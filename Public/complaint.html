关于投诉

本网站目前仅提供免费公开课，因此投诉管理仅在免费公开课中使用，收费课程的行为与本网站无直接关系，因此收费课程的投诉请直接与收费机构联系。产生的投诉由平台通过课堂回放来负责纠纷和仲裁。学员上完一课后，若对教学有异议，可在3日内通过网站投诉入口进行“投诉”。管理员收到投诉后，通过教师举证（举证期72小时）、课堂回放查看，在5个工作日（非周末和节假日）给出处理意见。

只要确认投诉有效，则被投诉的当堂课取消课时累计，并且所有参加课堂学习的学员（包括回放课）对此课堂教师的评分为1分。

投诉内容类别：

1.教学内容与课堂预告内容不符；

2.教学态度不认真，敷衍了事；

3.言语不文明规范（涉及侮辱/暴力/歧视/黄赌毒等）；

4.传播有害身心健康的思想和信息；

5.散布反动言论；

6.其他违反师德规范，造成不良影响和后果。

About Complaint

CIO offers free public classes currently and only complaint involved with these classes will be accepted and handled. The classes of other organizations which charge fees have nothing to do with CIO. If complaints with their classes occur, please contact them directly.

The complaint concerning with the CIO public classes will be judged by replaying the recorded class video. Learners are allowed to complain within 3 days after the class. CIO administrator is to deal with the complaint and reply within 5 working days after checking the recorded video and teacher’s proof (submitted within 72 hours).

If the complaint is verified as valid, the class complained will not be counted in. Rating from all learners, including learners of its recorded video, will be degraded to 1.

Complaint category:

1. Teaching content in the class is discrepant with its description in the forecast.

2. Teacher is half-hearted and perfunctory.

3. Uncivilized speech (insulting, violent, discriminatory or pornography-related)

4. Unwholesome ideology and information

5. Anti-social speech

6. Other inappropriate speech or behavior which violates teachers’ ethnics and causes adverse effect.
